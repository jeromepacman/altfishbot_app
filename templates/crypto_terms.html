Popular crypto terms

Altcoin / Alt

Any cyptocurrency other than Bitcoin

Alt season

An alt(s) season is a phase of the crypto market in which most altcoins have very high rally percentages.

ATH

All Time High, the highest price that an asset has achieved since its listing.

Bagholder

Someone with an amount of asset stucked at a very high price relative to the current price. He can't sell because of massive loss.

Bearish

Sentiment of decline (asset or market). Reference to the bear. A bear attacks with its claws from the top down.

Bullish

Sentiment of rise (asset or market). Reference to the bull. A bull attacks with its horns from the bottom up.

Break even

You have closed a position with no losses nor profits. You're "break even".

Breakout

When the price of a coin moves outside a defined zone or pattern such as support or resistance.

BTFD

Buy The Fucking Dip !

DYOR

Do your own research

FIAT

Fiduciary currency (like Dollar, Yen or Euro)

FOMO

Fear Of Missing Out

FUD

Fear, Uncertainty & Doubt

HODL

"hold on for dear life." The acronym originally came from a misspelling in a Bitcointalk forum

MOON

A currency that rises sharply in value

NGMI

"Not Going To Make It"

Panic sell

Global sell based on fear

Ponzi

Originally from Charles Ponzi, is a scam in which new investors funds are used to pay returns promised to previous investors

REKT

"rekt" slang for "wrecked." it means that a trader lost substantial amounts of money

Sat

The smallest amount of Bitcoin 0.00000001 (7 zeros) is called a Satoshi (sat).

Satoshi Nakamoto

Satoshi Nakamoto is the individual, or group of individuals, credited for founding the world’s first cryptocurrency, Bitcoin. The founder of Bitcoin remains completely anonymous.

Scam

A fraudulent scheme, typically for making quick profits.

SHILL (a coin)

A shiller is usually someone that has bought a a large bag of a particular crypto & tells to many people how great it is, in the hope that they buy it as well, in order to increases the demand then sell it high.

Swap

Exchange of a cryptocurrency without the need for a centralized market or other intermediaries.

TA

Technical Analyses

Whale

An individual or organization that holds a large amount of Bitcoin or other cryptocurrency, allowing them to impact the markets.

Weak Hands

Traders or investors that have lost the confidence to hold their assets are called weak hands.
